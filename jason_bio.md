## Short Bio
Jason Scarlett is a seasoned business leader with three decades of experience in the luxury residential and commercial construction market. As a Founding Partner of Hewn Elements, he spent eleven years advising clients on custom, high-specification solutions — developing deep expertise in premium product positioning, complex project sales cycles, and the consultative client relationships that characterize high-end procurement. His career established a consistent track record of bringing sophisticated buyers and best-in-class products together in markets where trust, expertise, and precision execution are non-negotiable.
