## Long Bio

Kyle McLeod is an experienced technology and operations leader with a passion for making complex technology accessible to the real work of non-tech businesses. He spent the first decade of his career in operations — spanning sales and marketing, business ops, people management, and process efficiency. The second decade has been defined by technology, working as a software engineer and consultant building complex systems and delivering streamlined user experiences wherever they're needed.
From financial technology, where a single mishandled edge case can cascade into massive financial exposure, to back-office process automation for home services businesses, Kyle brings an extreme focus on simplification. Software needs to work well for the end user and provide true value — not more complexity. That's why his number one goal, regardless of the project, is taking complex, multi-step processes and turning them into streamlined systems that fade into the background and just work.
That philosophy is why Kyle's consulting practice focuses on industrial and trades-driven businesses. He understands what it takes to digitize operations where the real expertise lives in people's heads, not in a database — and he knows that the fastest way to kill adoption is to make the solution more complicated than the problem. His approach is structured in layers: first capture the critical knowledge, then automate the repetitive workflows, then build the growth engine on top, with each phase delivering value before the next one starts.

## Short Bio

Kyle McLeod spent a decade in operations learning how businesses actually run, then spent the next decade building technology that makes them run better. As founder of Cloud99, he helps industrial and trades-driven businesses automate what slows them down, capture the knowledge that lives in people's heads, and build systems that simplify — turning messy, multi-step processes into streamlined tools that provide true value, not more overhead.
